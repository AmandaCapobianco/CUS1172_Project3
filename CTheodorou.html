Christiana Theodorou




Christiana Theodorou is a full-time Special Scientist for research at the Center for Applied Neuroscience (CAN), University of Cyprus. She currently works with Professor Georgia Panayiotou who is the director of the Clinical Psychology and Psychophysiology lab, part of the CAN, under a project which studies risk factors and prevention of gambling behaviour in young adults.

Christiana Theodorou completed her bachelor studies at the University of Cyprus in 2012 and a Master of Science in Psychological Research Methods at Lancaster University in UK in 2013. Now she is a Ph.D. student in Clinical Psychology at the University of Cyprus. Her research interests are mainly in the field of clinical psychology and especially focuses on emotional and behavioral deficits.
